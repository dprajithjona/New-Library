About

The Bishop Heber College Library serves as a catalyst, facilitating the user community's scholarly exploration, learning endeavours, and the generation of novel ideas. Its wealth of robust information resources dedicated to teaching, learning and research plays a pivotal role in connecting individuals with intellectual growth and innovative pursuits. Library’s current holdings include 1,28,779 books, 555 rare books, 222 print periodicals, 1917 non-book materials, 479 theses, 10 newspapers, 31,35,000+ e-books and 6000+ e-journals. The subscribed electronic resources can be accessed from on and off campus. The Library is fully automated using NIRMALS & KOHA Open Source Library Management Software integrated with RFID technology, Self-Service Kiosk & Web OPAC.
